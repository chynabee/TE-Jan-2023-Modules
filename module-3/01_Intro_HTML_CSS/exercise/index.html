Who is Diddy?

His real name is Sean "Puffy" Combs. He is an American rapper, producer, and owner of Bad Boy Record Label. He has recently formed a new record label called LOVE RECORDS, which is named after his newest addition to the family, Baby Girl Love Combs. He has been making hit records since early 1996.
